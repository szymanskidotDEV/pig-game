GAME RULES:

The game has 2 players, playing in rounds

In each turn, a player rolls 2 dices as many times as he whishes. Each result get added to his ROUND score

BUT, if the player rolls a 1 at least in one dice, all his ROUND score gets lost. After that, it's the next player's turn

The player can choose to 'Hold', which means that his ROUND score gets added to his GLOBAL score. After that, it's the next player's turn

The first player to reach 100 points (final score by default is 100) on GLOBAL score wins the game, players can change the "final score" during the game

A player looses his ENTIRE score when he rolls in one dice a 6, 2 times in a row. After that, it's the next player's turn.

Press any key to continue..
